3. Testing Document

The goal of our questions were to find out how easy it was to navigate and to find the information you were looking for. We thought that the best way to get many answers was by having a short test with few questions and divide the test in three parts, the first asking for information about the tester, the second part rating the website from 1-6 with a numbers of questions. The last part is more open, where we ask the tester for things we could have done different and things they liked.
